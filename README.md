# 🏷️ QR-Genie
A simple and sleek Flutter app that lets you generate custom QR codes with your own colors. 

Perfect for small businesses, events, or anyone who wants their QR code to stand out from the boring black-and-white standard.


## ✨ What it does
* **Create QR Codes:** Type in any link or text, and it instantly makes a QR code.
* **Pick Your Colors:** Change the colors to match your style.
* **Save to Gallery:** One tap to save the final high-res image directly to your phone.


## 🛠️ Built With
* **Flutter** (Dart)
* **Packages:** `qr_flutter` (for the QR magic), `image_gallery_saver_plus` (for saving to your phone).
